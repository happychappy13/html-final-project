About Me

I'm Amanda Chappell and I love group fitness! I have been teaching classes since 2015. I am licensed in Zumba, Aqua Zumba, Zumba Gold, SilverSneakers Classic, and SilverSneakers Yoga. I love learning new things and helping others in their fitness journeys. That is why I created this page. I want to help you reach your goals! Here you will find tips and tricks to help you become more confident in building an at home workout that fits your needs. I will include workout ideas and tutorials for proper technique to keep you safe and physically active.
